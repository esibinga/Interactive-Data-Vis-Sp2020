Climate Change Indicators: Temperature Extremes

Where are "unusually hot" days becoming more frequent?


How do I interpret the "Change" number?

"Unusually hot" means temperatures hotter than the 95th percentile temperatures at that location from 1948-2015. A change of 32.14 means that there are now about 32 more days per year in that location that are hotter than 5% of hottest days from 1948-2015. A change of -16.18 means that the location is seeing about 16 fewer days per year that reach the historic 5% hottest mark. Data is sourced from The Environmental Protection Agency.
